Welcome to rebellr

Rebellr is a website where you can interact with your classmates at your school in an unprecedented way.

With rebellr, you can:

  • Search for people in your school,
  • See what classes your friends are taking,
  • Find different clubs in your school,
  • Discover different events in your school (such as football games),
  • And much more!

To get started, either login or register below. You must have an @bvsd.org email to register.
